Who I am

I'm a data scientist and ML engineer based in Kampala, Uganda, with a BSc in Electrical Engineering from Makerere University and an ongoing MSc in AI & Data Science at UTAMU. I bridge the gap between engineering rigour and business insight.

At the Makerere AI Health Lab, I design analytical workflows for public health early-warning systems. Previously at Marconi Innovations Lab, I built and deployed real-time ML models for agricultural and operational research.

My work spans the full data stack — from ETL pipelines and warehousing through to Power BI dashboards and explainable ML models. I care about making data actionable, auditable, and accessible to any stakeholder.
